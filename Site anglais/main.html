FAQ

We offer a wide range of water activities suitable for all skill levels, including jet skiing, paddleboarding, kayaking, tubing, water skiing, wakeboarding, and boat excursions. Both guided and unguided options are available.

We highly recommend booking in advance, especially during the high season (July–August), to ensure availability and your preferred time slot. You can book online or by phone.

The minimum age varies depending on the activity. For example, children as young as 6 can enjoy paddleboarding or kayaking with adult supervision, while jet skiing is available from age 16 with parental permission. Check each activity page for details.
